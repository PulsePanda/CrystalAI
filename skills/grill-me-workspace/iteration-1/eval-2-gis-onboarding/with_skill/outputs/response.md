Let's start with the most load-bearing piece of this whole workflow: the trigger.

You said "when HR emails me a new hire." Who is "me" here — is HR emailing your GIS address directly, or is there a dedicated inbox this should route through? And more importantly: what does that email actually look like? Is it a structured form submission with consistent fields, or is it a human-written email where someone types "hey we hired Sarah Johnson, she starts Monday, 3rd grade teacher"?

The answer to that completely changes the parsing strategy — and whether this workflow is even reliable enough to run unattended.
