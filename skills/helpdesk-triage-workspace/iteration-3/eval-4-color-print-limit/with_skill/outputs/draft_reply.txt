Hey Heidi,

You've hit your monthly color print quota — that's what the "limit" error means. The good news is you can still print in black and white in the meantime.

One heads-up: even if a document looks black and white, it'll count against your color quota unless you explicitly select "black and white" in the print dialog. So make sure that's set when you print from here on out this month.

Limits reset automatically on the 1st of each month. If you need it reset sooner and have a good reason, that goes through the designated approver on our end — just let me know and I can route that for you.

Let me know if you run into anything else!
